# AI_switches

###
#
# Each country belongs to an AI category, determining its construction scheme and production queues, roughly according to
# approximate tech level at a given point of time. West_europe and east_europe categories refer to democratic/nationalist
# and communist countries respectively, not necessarily having strict geographic meaning.
#
# There are following special rules that enable alternative AIs:
#
# 1. If Nationalist China wins the civil war (or unites with communists peacefully), CHI_united_XXXX.ai files are used since 1950.
# 2. If Japan falls under Soviet or Chinese occupation and influence, respectively, JAP_SOV_XXXX.ai and JAP_CHI_XXXX.ai files are used.
# 3. If Germany is united peacefully as a neutral state, GER_neutral_XXXX.ai files are used, since 1950. Note that historically neutral
#	 Austria uses normal AUS_XXXX.ai file and only if it is non-neutral, it either gets standard west_europe AI, or east_europe, in case
#	 of a communist tag.
# 4. In case of WW3 firing, several countries get special AI files, aimed at more aggresive invasions and front options and other 
#	 characteristics for: USA (builds more rockets and transport vessels, constructs much less), FRA (stops construction, concentrates on
#	 land divisions), ENG (stops construction, builds more aircrafts and transport vessels, constructs much less), DFR and DDR
#	 (stop construction, concentrate on land divisions), CHI and CHC (construct less and have lower neutrality, since 1950),
#	 JAP (stop construction, starts to build military), SOV (constructs much less, focuses on navy and land divisions).
# 5. Some countries receive special AI that is meant to be active during times of prescripted wars. Wars that are covered are:
#	 - Chinese Civil War: CHI and CHC get their special AI for the start of the war (CHI_1947.ai and CHC_1947.ai) and if the war ends 
#	   before 1950, CHI_1949.ai and CHC_1949.ai are loaded. If Nationalists win before 1950, CHI_1945.ai is loaded instead;
#	 - Korean War: KOR and PRK use their standard 1950 AI at the beginning of the war. When KOR is meant to counterattack, 1951 AI is loaded,
#	   when stalemate happens 1952 AI is loaded; finally after the war ends, AIs are reverted to KOR_1950.ai and PRK_1950.ai (and non-
#	   aggression pact enforces peace).
#
###
